Hello there!

Welcome to the wonderful world of Borgosity, where knowledge assimilation is the one true constant!

My name is Phill and I make digital things with words (aka: games programmer). Although I wasn’t always this way; I was once a drone in a large corporation doing repetitive mundane work. Then one day I woke up and remembered how much fun I had at university doing graphics and games programming and thought “I wish I could do that for a living!”. So I applied to the Academy of Interactive Entertainment to learn how to become a games programmer…

And here we are two years later, I moved from Canberra to Melbourne and having having heaps of fun making games and meeting like minded people.

So now that we’re fast becoming friends, please feel free to check out my current projects on Instagram , Twitter and YouTube and hopefully you find something of interest!
